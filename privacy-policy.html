Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Moxy ("we", "us", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. By using Moxy, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Moxy stores the following data exclusively on your device using local storage. This data is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Your first name (provided during onboarding)
  • Anxiety type selections, frequency, triggers, and goals
  • Mood check-in history and mood entries
  • Journal entries
  • DARE session and grounding session completion records
  • App preferences (notification settings, reminder time, language)

We do not have access to this data. It exists solely on your device and is not backed up to any server operated by us.

1.2 Subscription and Purchase Data

If you purchase a subscription or in-app purchase, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat, a third-party service, to manage subscription status and entitlements. RevenueCat may collect:

  • An anonymous user identifier (generated automatically, not linked to your personal identity)
  • Purchase and subscription status information
  • Device type and operating system version

We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

1.3 Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, notification scheduling is handled locally on your device using the operating system's notification system. We do not use push notification servers and do not collect notification tokens.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • We do not collect personal health or medical data
  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not collect analytics or usage tracking data
  • We do not use cookies or web tracking technologies
  • We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, or contact information
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • We do not share or sell any data to third parties

3. How We Use Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Provide personalized app experiences (e.g., greeting you by name, tailoring DARE exercises to your selected mood)
  • Display your mood and journal history within the app
  • Track your progress over time
  • Schedule local notification reminders at your chosen time

4. Data Storage and Security

All personal data is stored locally on your device using the operating system's secure storage mechanisms. Since we do not transmit or store your data on external servers, your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device encryption and passcode/biometric locks.

If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — Subscription management. Processes anonymous purchase data. Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store — Payment processing. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

6. Children's Privacy

Moxy is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Delete your data: You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the App. Since all data is stored locally, uninstalling permanently removes it.
  • Notifications: You can enable or disable notifications at any time through the App's settings or your device's system settings.
  • Subscriptions: You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID settings in the App Store.
